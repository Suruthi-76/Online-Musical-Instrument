Frequently Asked Questions - EMI Options

Q: What are the available EMI options for musical instruments? A: We offer 6-month, 12-month, and 24-month EMI plans.
Q: Is there any interest charged on the EMI plans? A: Yes, there is a nominal interest rate applied to the EMI plans.
Q: Can I prepay the EMI amount without any penalty? A: Yes, you can prepay the EMI amount without any penalty.
Q: What documents are required to avail the EMI option? A: You need to provide proof of identity, address, and income to avail the EMI option.
Q: How is the EMI amount deducted from my account? A: The EMI amount is automatically deducted from your linked bank account on the specified date each month.
Q: Can I change my EMI plan after availing it? A: No, the EMI plan once selected cannot be changed.
Q: Is there a processing fee for availing the EMI option? A: Yes, a small processing fee is applicable while availing the EMI option.
Q: What happens if I miss an EMI payment? A: Missing an EMI payment may lead to additional charges and impact your credit score.
Q: Can I avail EMI options on all musical instruments? A: EMI options are available on most musical instruments, but it's best to check with our customer service for specific details.
Q: How can I cancel my EMI plan? A: To cancel your EMI plan, you need to contact our customer support team.
