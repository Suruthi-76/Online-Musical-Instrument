Shipping Policy

IMPORTANT SHIPPING UPDATE:

FEE: Bajaao has always maintained a free standard shipping policy to the majority of the locations but unfortunately, due to the ongoing Corona pandemic this policy has had to be changed with effect May 6, 2020. This is because courier partners are encountering significantly higher operations costs shipping to most red/orange/green zones, and are passing on these costs to Bajaao.
1)As a result, we are charging a portion of these costs to customers via a modest shipping fee on every order placed on our website until further notice.
2) We appreciate your understanding and cooperation during this difficult time. Please be assured that we are doing everything possible to ensure that your product reaches you in a timely manner.
3)We are also ensuring your safety by disinfecting all packages before, during, and after packing.
